'Alt'+'Enter' - Toggle between windowed and full screen

'Escape' - Return to main menu

'Shift' - Run faster

'Space' - Jump

WASD - Left/right movement and climb/drop from ledge

For cooking, hold 'Space' and release when food is cooked but not burnt

Offer refugees ingredients from your inventory with E.
If you have the correct ingredient, refugee will be cured.

State persists even when returning to main menu.
State is lost when the game is closed.

Debug:

'Left Ctrl' - Turn on debug window

'R' - Reset player location in scene (while in debug)

'P' - Turn off wall occlusion (increased performance)

'L' - Turn off shadows (increased performance)
